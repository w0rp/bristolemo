Live Performance!

On 11th January at 21:00, Mr. Wolf's will be hosting an incredible Emo music event that is not to be missed. Titled "You Will Listen To Us," this event will feature two talented bands, Leonardo Lemuel & Gunkus, who are sure to captivate the audience with their raw and emotional performances. Both bands have a unique sound that combines elements of punk, indie, and emo, creating a truly unforgettable experience. Fans of the genre will be blown away by the energy and passion these bands bring to the stage. So mark your calendars and get ready to be swept away by the powerful and moving music of Leonardo Lemuel & Gunkus at this must-see event in Bristol.

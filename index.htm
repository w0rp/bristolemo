Who are you?

We are a rag-tag group of musicians in Bristol. We love making music together. Andrew Wray is the primary maintainer of this website, and loves to promote Emo, Punk, epunk and more! All-in-all, we are:

  • Andrew Wray (Leonardo Lemuel)
  • Patrick Fenton (Gunkus)
  • Chris Beale (Chris Vox)
  • Mike Owen (everyone's friend)
  • Together... "Corporate Retreat!" (Our punk band in Bristol)
  • And various other acts...

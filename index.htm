Where am I?

Welcome to the front page of Emo in Bristol.

Who are you?

"We happy few", are a band of brothers. We hope to create great music in Bristol, and do what little we can to share the best genre of music. (Okay, it's pretty good at least.)

We are:

  • Andrew Wray (Leonardo Lemuel)
  • Patrick Fenton (Gunkus)
  • Chris Beale (Chris Vox)
  • James Goodyer
  • Together... "Corporate Retreat!" (Our band)
